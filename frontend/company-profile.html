A&H Electromechanical & Digital Systems PLC

Company Profile

A&H Electromechanical Works PLC is a licensed and VAT-registered electromechanical contracting company established on 5 June 2022 in Ethiopia. The company delivers high-quality electromechanical, electrical, HVAC, and industrial maintenance services for public and private sector clients.

Core Activities

  • Electromechanical installation
  • Electrical installation & maintenance
  • HVAC installation
  • Industrial mechanical systems
  • Water pipe installation
  • Fire alarm systems
